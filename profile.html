人物简介Biography

邝玲玲（西莉拉可·邝，ศิริลักษณ์ คอง）为港泰混血，父亲来自香港，母亲来自泰国加拉信府。她在香港长大并完成小学与部分中学教育，十七岁回到泰国继续高中与大学学业，2018 年自孔敬大学国际学院旅游管理系毕业。 LingLing (Sirilak Kwong, ศิริลักษณ์ คอง) is of Hong Kong–Thai descent. Her father is from Hong Kong and her mother from Kalasin, Thailand. She grew up in Hong Kong, returned to Thailand at 17 to finish high school, and graduated from Khon Kaen University International College (Tourism Management) in 2018.

2018 年获得孔敬 Khao Suan Kwang 选美冠军后，2019 年参加 Channel 3 试镜并签约，随后开始电视与主持工作。她在 2020 年以《直到天空迎来太阳》出道，后续出演多部受关注的电视剧并参演综艺与杂志拍摄。 After winning the Khao Suan Kwang pageant in 2018, she auditioned for Channel 3 and signed in 2019. She debuted in the 2020 drama Until the Sky’s the Sun and later appeared in notable series, variety shows and magazine features.

代表角色包括《亲爱的玛卡莉》中的 Theerak、《我们的秘密》里的 Fahlada、《只有你》中的 Tawan 等。她也参与电视剧原声带演唱与粉丝见面会活动。 Notable roles include Theerak in The Dear Lady, Fahlada in The Secret of Us, and Tawan in Only You. She has also contributed to drama soundtracks and performed at fan meetings.
